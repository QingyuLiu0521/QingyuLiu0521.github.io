Biography

 I work as a research assistant at SJTU X-LANCE Lab, supervised by Prof. Xie Chen. Prior to this, I completed my undergraduate studies at Shanghai Normal University, supervised by Prof. Yanhua Long. Currently, I am applying for Master's programs in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) in the United States, aiming to start in Fall 2025.

I work in the field of Audio, Generative model, Machine learning, and Deep learning. Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Speech Synthesis (Text to Speech)
  • Multimodal Large Language Model
  • Sound Event Detection
I'm a Manchester United fanatic, and my dream is to see them conquer the Premier League once more. Feel free to reach out if you want to talk footy!😊😊
